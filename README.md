# RedLightGreenLight

Play a RLGL with Cozmo. Cozmo is the judge and he uses his cubes as the light signals. This is a two player game for kids who can play it using their fingers to walk during the green light. The players must try and reach their respective cubes first and tap it to win.

# Dependencies

Apart from the cozmo module, this program depends on OpenCV and Numpy.
